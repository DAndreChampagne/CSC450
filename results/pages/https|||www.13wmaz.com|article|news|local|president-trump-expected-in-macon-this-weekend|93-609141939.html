Here's how you can sign up to see President Trump in Macon

President Trump is expected in Central Georgia this weekend to campaign for Brian Kemp
Author: WMAZ Staff
Published: 10:56 AM EDT October 29, 2018
Updated: 8:58 PM EDT October 29, 2018

UPDATE: President Donald Trump's visit to Macon has now been officially confirmed by the White House.

He will be coming to Macon to campaign with Brian Kemp -- the Republican candidate for governor.

He will be at the North Hangar of the Middle Georgia Regional Airport at 100 East Dr. on Sunday, Nov. 4.

Doors open at 1 p.m. and the event begins at 4 p.m.

If you are planning on attending, you can register for up to 2 tickets per phone number.
