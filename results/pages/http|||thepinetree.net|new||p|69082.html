President Donald J. Trump Signs America’s Water Infrastructure Act of 2018, Into Law

By admin on October 23, 2018 10:38 pm -No Comment

Share on Facebook Follow on Facebook Add to Google+ Connect on Linked in Subscribe by Email Print This Post

Washington, DC…On Tuesday, October 23, 2018, the President signed into law: S. 3021, the “America’s Water Infrastructure Act of 2018,” which authorizes construction of Army Corps of Engineers water resources projects for flood risk management, navigation, hurricane and storm damage risk reduction, and environmental restoration; modifies previously authorized projects; and contains other water-related provisions.
