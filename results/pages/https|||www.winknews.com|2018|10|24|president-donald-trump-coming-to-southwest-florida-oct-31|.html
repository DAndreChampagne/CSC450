Trump ‘MAGA’ rally could impact trick-or-treating & traffic in SWFL

President Donald Trump is expected to come to Southwest Florida on Wednesday, October 31 to headline a MAGA campaign rally.

According to McClatchy DC Bureau, The Make America Great Again rally is intended to gather support for gubernatorial candidate Ron DeSantis and other republicans before the upcoming midterm elections on November 6. It’s unclear if Gov. Rick Scott will attend the event.

DeSantis faces democratic opponent Andrew Gillum in the polls.

“We are pleased to announce the next stop on our national midterm campaign tour with a Make America Great Again rally on Wednesday, October 31st in Fort Myers, Florida, featuring President Donald Trump,” said Michael Glassner Chief Operating Officer to Donald J. Trump for President, Inc.

Wednesday, October 31st, 2018, 7:00 PM (EDT): Fort Myers, FL
President Trump will hold an event at the Hertz Arena in Fort Myers, FL.
Hertz Arena
11000 Everblades Parkway
Estero, FL 33928

Doors open at 4:00 PM (EDT) for General Admission.
