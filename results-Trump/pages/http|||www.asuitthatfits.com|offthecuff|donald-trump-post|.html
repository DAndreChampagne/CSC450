Donald Trump

May 18, 2011 By Anthony Silver

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google+
  • Pinterest

With the 57th US presidential election set for November 2012, campaigns are already being launched to gain early advantages in the voting stakes.

Whilst the respective party representatives aren’t to be decided until later this year, some front runners are already emerging for the spot as President, including the incumbent Barack Obama and, quite surprisingly, billionaire businessman Donald Trump, although Mr Trump has since withdrawn…

The two have already traded a few metaphorical blows since the entrepreneur announced that he may run for the Republican Party a few weeks back, with Trump even claiming that the current President wasn’t born in the US. Although this rumour was finally quashed with Obama releasing his birth certificate, it is fair to say that Trump, who shot to public fame as ‘the boss’ in the US version of The Apprentice, is ruffling a few feathers and gaining some political momentum across the pond.

A father of five, and married to his third wife, Melania, Trump is known for his straight talking and rather brash attitude, a style that he mirrors in his choice of business clothing. Plain, dark suits  contrasting with bright ties are his choice of attire whenever seen on camera, a formula that he rarely strays from.
