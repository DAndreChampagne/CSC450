Amir condoles with US President

 19 Nov 2018 - 9:17

QNA

DOHA: Amir H H Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani sent yesterday a cable of condolences to the President of the United States, Donald Trump, on the victims of the wildfires in California, wishing speedy recovery for the injured.

Deputy Amir H H Sheikh Abdullah bin Hamad Al Thani also sent a cable of condolences to the President of the United States, Donald Trump, on the victims of the wildfires in California, wishing speedy recovery for the injured.

Prime Minister and Interior Minister H E Sheikh Abdullah bin Nasser bin Khalifa Al Thani also sent a cable of condolences to the President on the victims of the wildfires, wishing speedy recovery for the injured.
