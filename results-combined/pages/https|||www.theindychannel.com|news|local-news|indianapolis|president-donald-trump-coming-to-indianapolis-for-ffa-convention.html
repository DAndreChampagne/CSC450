President Donald Trump coming to address youth at National FFA Convention in Indianapolis

INDIANAPOLIS -- President Donald Trump will be in Indianapolis this weekend for the National FFA Convention.

Kristy Meyer, the spokesperson for the National FFA Organization, said Trump will be in town on Saturday, but couldn't elaborate more on his visit.

The National FFA Convention and Expo is one of the largest student conventions of the year and draws more than 65,000 students to downtown Indianapolis. 

The other details about President Trump's visit have not been released.
