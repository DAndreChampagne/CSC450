March 24, 2017

President Donald J. Trump Enacts Driving Restrictions in the U.S.

After recently speaking with Prime Minister Theresa May of the United Kingdom, President Donald J. Trump has learned that a car was used in a terrorist attack in London, killing four people and injuring dozens more.

Therefore, effective immediately, the President is placing restrictions on the driving of cars in America.

If you are Muslim, you are no longer allowed to drive a car, since the attacks in London were believed to be an act of Islam-inspired terrorism.

If you are a woman, you are no longer allowed to drive a car. None of the attackers were women, but the President feels strongly that ladies should not drive cars.

If you are Mexican, you are not allowed to drive a car. Yes, the President realizes that Mexico had nothing to do with the London attack, but “you can never be too careful”.

If you are gay, you are no longer allowed to drive a car. The President understands that the attackers in London were not gay, but he wants to put restrictions on gay people “just in case”.

If you have ever provided birth control to ladies or given someone an abortion, you are no longer allowed to drive a car, because you’re probably a terrorist.

If you’re a Democrat, you’re not allowed to drive a car, because you’re obviously in cahoots with Bernie Sanders and/or Hillary Clinton.

If you’re Rachel Maddow, even though you’ve already been banned from driving a car because you’re gay AND a woman, you’re not allowed to drive a car, motorcycle, truck, van, or scooter, and you’re not allowed to ride a bicycle, either. You’re also not allowed to be a passenger in a car.

Thank you for your cooperation in this matter.
