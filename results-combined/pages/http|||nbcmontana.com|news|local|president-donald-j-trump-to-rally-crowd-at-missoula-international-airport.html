    President Donald J. Trump to rally crowd at Missoula International Airport

    by Heidi Meili

    President Trump to hold rally at Neptune Aviation Services.


    MISSOULA, Mont.

    President Trump is returning to Montana for a third time.

    Matt Rosendale campaign staff members are confirming that the President will rally a crowd at a hangar operated by Neptune Aviation Services at the Missoula International Airport.

    The rally is scheduled to get underway Thursday, October 18th at 6:30 p.m.

    It is free to attend the rally, but the Matt Rosendale campaign asks people to register and arrive early.

    President Trump has visited Billings and Great Falls earlier this year, to campaign for Rosendale, as he challenges Jon Tester for his seat in the U.S. Senate.
