Knowlan Society Faculty Director

Dr. Daphne Keshishian 

Birthplace: Elizabeth, NJ

Undergraduate school: University of Pennsylvania

Medical school: Georgetown University School of Medicine

Residency/Fellowship: Internal Medicine, Mount Sinai Medical Center, NY, NY

Current Position: Internist at Bethesda Medical Associates, Bethesda MD,   Medical Alumni Board, GUSM, Preceptor, Ambulatory Care, Executive Board, Women’s Board, American Heart Association,

Honors and awards: Georgetown Clinical Society: Doctor of the Year, 2015; GUSM: Founder’s Award, 2017

Medical interest:  Preventive Medicine, Culinary Medicine

Outside interests: I live in Potomac MD with my husband Lee and three children.  I enjoy Yoga and Pilates, blogging at diningwithadoc.com, golf and cooking.
